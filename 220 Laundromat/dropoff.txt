Laundry drop-off and delivery services offer a convenient solution for managing your laundry needs. These services typically involve the following steps:

	1. Scheduling a Pickup: You can schedule a pickup time that suits your convenience, either through a website or an app.
	2. Drop-off: You leave your laundry at a designated location or have it picked up from your home.
	3. Cleaning Process: The laundry service washes, dries, and folds your clothes, often using high-quality and eco-friendly cleaning products.
	4. Delivery: Your clean laundry is delivered back to your doorstep, usually within a specified timeframe.
