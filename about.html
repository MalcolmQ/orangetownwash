About Us

A family‑owned laundromat serving Orange, NJ with care, quality, and convenience.

Family-Owned & Community Focused

As a family business, Orange Town Wash understands how important it is to get your laundry done quickly, affordably, and with care. Located on South Center Street in Orange, NJ, we offer a cost‑effective way to wash and dry your clothes using reliable, modern machines.

Founded by Pooran Praimlal, our laundromat was built on the belief that everyday tasks should be easier for busy families. We aim to meet the laundry needs of every customer with warmth, respect, and consistent service.

Why Wash With Us?

At Orange Town Wash, we’re committed to making your laundry experience comfortable and convenient. We may not be a large commercial laundromat, but we offer a warm, peaceful, and inviting environment.

A helpful attendant is always available to answer questions, assist with machines, and resolve any issues quickly. Enjoy complimentary coffee while you wait, or relax with our in‑store TV.

We offer a variety of washers and dryers in multiple sizes to accommodate different load types — ensuring your laundry is done efficiently and on time.
